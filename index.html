VET EVASION

HOW TO PLAY: Use 'w' = UP, 'a' = LEFT, 's' = DOWN, 'd' = = RIGHT keys to move!

HOW TO WIN: Don't get caught by the hands! If your score is -2 or less, you will be going to the VET. All you need to do is stall and eat treats for 30 seconds. Treats will increase your score by 1, and hands will decrease by 1.
